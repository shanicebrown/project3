Invaild

Filmed in Kansas City, Missouri, native artist Invalid, expresses his artist vision for his song "Ancient Rules". The lyrics speak of overcoming adversity and staying true to oneself. Invalid enlisted the clothing esthetics of Sew Unrelaxed to connect with Ancient Rules. Overall the style came together to translate very tribal, and Warrior fierceness that is The Woman.

Sew Unrelaxed Chief Designer, Missy IsaMoore, designed and styled the models for this shoot. Coordinating the revealing mid-drifts, geometric design, animal-esque print, partnered with bright orange, the contrast aligned perfectly with the very green outdoors and rugged sound of the songs lyrics.
